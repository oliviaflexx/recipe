Grocery List Making Made Easy

Step 1

Add your ingredients

Simply type in your ingredients and click the plus button to add them to your ingredients

Step 2

Based on what ingredients you have, we find recipes that best match what you have. From there, you can click on the plus button to add a recipe that you want to make to your grocery list. You can also like and dislike recipes so you don't forget how you feel about them when making grocery lists in the future. Click on the three dots if you'd like more information about the recipe like the amount of time it takes and how many calories it is. Narrow the recipes down to exactly what you're looking for using our filters and preferences.

Step 3

We create a grocery list for the recipes you chose. We tell you exactly how much of each ingredient you need so no more need to pull out your calculator or forget anything!
